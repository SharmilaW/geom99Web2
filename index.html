Public Transportation Routing in Lindsay, Ontario

Four GIS students at Sir Sandford Fleming College hoping to make taking public transit in Lindsay, Ontario, easier for everyone.

Athulya Sabu

Helen Plesko

Rahul Saravanabavan

Sharmila Wagle

Problem Statement

Ideal

A user-friendly public web application that will help the general public navigate around Lindsay, Ontario, by public transportation.

Reality

Members of the rural community like Lindsay do not have quick access to public transit routes on common navigation apps like Google Maps.

Consequences

User error may include the public getting lost or missing their bus stop. The study area for this project is Lindsay, Ontario, however, the application can be adapted at a later date to consider other areas without public transportation maps.

Proposal

Finding technologies that are required to make a user-friendly public web application that will help the general public navigate rural areas like Lindsay through public transportation.

Technology Used

ArcGIS Hub, ArcGIS Experience Builder, ArcGIS Online, Open Source
